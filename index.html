INN 2024

A scene of an Inn in the forest. I created this project to use it as a reason to learn Unreal Engine 5.

The whole scene was constructed in Unreal Engine 5.

The inn and the horse stable were created with modular pieces modeled and textured in Blender.



WINTER 2024

I got the idea for a winter scene when I was snow boarding in Levi. In this project I wanted to try a first-person perspective and making a bigger environment to observe. The hardest part was to fake the scale of the background to make it look bigger than it actually is. I used Blender for modelling all the assets for the scenes, then I textured the assets with Material Maker and CLIP Studio Paint, and finally build the scene in Unity.

SKY ISLAND 2023

This is a personal project that I started to build because I wanted to deepen my knowledge with Unity. Everything that is visible is made by me. First I designed an image of the scene in Blender to know what was the core idea and scope of the project. I wanted to keep it as small as possible but visually interesting. After I had modelled and textured all the 3D models in Blender and Material Maker I constructed the scene in Unity. During that time I had an idea to add an airplane so the player can navigate the scene.

RIVER 2023 - 2024

While working with the Sky Island-project I got an idea for another small scene project. I wanted to combine a simple movement style with a small scene. This time it was rowing a boat. First I designed an image of the scene in Blender, textured all the models with Material Maker or CLIP Studio Paint. Then I constructed the scene in unity and made a simple code for the movement. Finally I added fishes, herons and small flies to the scene to make it more lively.
