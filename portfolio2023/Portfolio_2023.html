STUDIO ISORÄTTYÄ 2021 - 2023

At Studio Isorattya I worked as a visual designer which included modeling art pieces and sketches with 3D softwares. In addition I designed a website, an artbook, cards, t-shirts and mechanical 3D parts for printing. My main tools were Blender, Solidworks and Rhino for creating the art pieces.

VRKIWI

At VRKiwi I worked as a texture artist for their project called CAVE DIGGER 2 - Dig Harder. I textured various buildings in the Hub world and some of the secret collectibles on the side. I used Substance Painter for texturing the objects and Unity for testing the materials in the scenes.


RESISTANCE GAMES

At Resistance Games I worked as a 3D artist. I was responsible of creating decorative objects and texturing them for various levels. Here are a couple of screenshots of the levels I made. I used Blender to create the meshes, then textured them in Substance Painter and finally placed them to the scene in Unreal Engine.
